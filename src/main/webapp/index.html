Welcome to Fermata

Fermata simplifies testing and evaluating the email capabilities of software.

  • Any email messages sent here will be saved and displayed.
  • Test workflows involving multiple people, without checking someone else's mailbox.
  • Verify only the emails that should be sent actually are.
